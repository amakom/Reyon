About Reyon

Crafting premium water,
one drop at a time.

Reyon exists to bring clean, healthy drinking water to humanity. Through advanced reverse osmosis technology and uncompromising quality control, we craft water that's pure in source, purposeful in design, and powerful in what it adds to your day.

Our Story

Built on a simple, uncompromising idea.

Water touches every part of how we live — how we feel, perform, recover, and show up. We started Reyon because we believed Nigeria deserved a water brand that took that responsibility seriously: from source to sip.

Today, our state-of-the-art facilities in Lagos and Warri produce premium drinking water that meets — and often exceeds — international purity standards. Every bottle is laboratory-tested. Every batch is verified. Every detail is by design.

How We Refine

Three stages. Zero compromise.

Every drop passes through a meticulously engineered purification chain before it ever reaches your bottle.

01

Carbon Filtration

Activated carbon filters strip impurities, chlorine and organic compounds — preserving the water's natural integrity. This first stage clears the path for everything that follows.

02

Demineralization

Reverse osmosis technology removes dissolved minerals and contaminants at the molecular level for unmatched clarity. This is where Reyon's signature crispness is engineered.

03

UV & Ozone Disinfection

Ultraviolet light and ozone gas eliminate microorganisms — leaving water that's safe, sterile and ready to drink. The final guarantee before sealing.
