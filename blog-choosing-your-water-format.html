Choosing your Reyon format.

Guide 3 min read Reyon Editorial

Four sizes. Four moments. Picking the right Reyon for the situation isn't complicated — but a quick frame helps. Here's how we'd guide it.

50CL — The personal companion

The smallest, lightest, most pocket-friendly option. Best for the moments hydration is reactive: a school run, a meeting in transit, the gym, the car. Buy it when you want a single serving you can finish without setting it down.

If you regularly forget to drink water during the day, 50CL bottles are a great forcing function — every empty one is a visible "I drank that" signal.

75CL — The everyday upgrade

The size most people end up reaching for most often. A little more substantial than 50CL, easier to share, ideal for events, workouts, and any situation where one bottle should comfortably last an hour or two.

It's our best seller for a reason — it sits in the sweet spot between portable and enough.

The right format isn't the biggest or the smallest. It's the one that fits the moment without thinking about it.

1.5 Litres — The home and office bottle

This is the workhorse. Sized for the dining table, the desk, and the road trip. Buy 1.5L when you want to drink continuously throughout the day without constant refills.

One bottle covers a meaningful percentage of an adult's recommended daily intake — making it the simplest way to track and meet your goal.

19L — The dispenser format

For when you're not buying water for yourself — you're buying it for a team, a household, a congregation, a customer base. The 19L refillable dispenser bottle is built for shared spaces:

  • Offices and co-working spaces
  • Churches and event venues
  • Malls and supermarkets
  • Large family homes

It's also the most cost-efficient format per litre. If you're going through more than 30 litres a week, 19L is almost always the right answer.

Quick decision guide

  • One person, one moment → 50CL
  • Active or social occasion → 75CL
  • One person, full day → 1.5L
  • A whole space, every day → 19L

Still unsure? Tell us how you'll use it and we'll point you to the right size — and the right quantity.
